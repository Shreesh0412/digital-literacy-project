# Stay Safe Online: Identity Theft Prevention Checklist
## Account & Password Security:
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Never share OTPs or verification codes with anyone
## Safe Browsing Practices:
- Always verify emails, messages, and links before clicking
- Be cautious of messages creating urgency or fear
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive activities
## Personal Information Safety:
- Do not share personal information such as Aadhaar number, bank details, or passwords on unknown websites
- Log out from shared or public devices after use
## Financial & Account Monitoring:
- Regularly check bank statements and account activity
- Immediately report any suspicious transactions
## Device Security:
- Keep your devices and applications updated with the latest security patches

# Help and Reporting:
## If you are a victim of cybercrime:
- Report online in the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the National Cyber Crime Helpline Number: 1930
